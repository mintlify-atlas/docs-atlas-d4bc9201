## Key features

### Image generation
Access 58 Stable Diffusion and 8 SDXL models to generate realistic and creative images from text prompts. Choose from models like PhotoPerfect for photorealistic results or specialized models for unique artistic styles.

### Language models
Leverage powerful LLMs including ChatGPT, Gemini, Gemma, and KellyAI for text generation, Q&A, summarization, translation, and conversational AI.

### Image processing
Enhance images with AI-powered upscaling (up to 4x), background removal, image-to-image transformation, and image captioning.

### Voice capabilities
Generate natural-sounding audio from text with 49 different voice models, or transcribe spoken audio to text with high accuracy across multiple languages.

### Developer-friendly
- **Async/await support**: Built on `aiohttp` for high-performance asynchronous operations
- **Simple API**: Clean, intuitive methods with sensible defaults
- **Type hints**: Full typing support for better IDE integration
- **Base64 encoding**: Easy handling of binary media data
